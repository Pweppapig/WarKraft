IMPORTANT PLUGINS: Crackshot (free) https://www.spigotmc.org/resources/crackshot-guns.48301/ & Crackshot Plus (paid) https://www.spigotmc.org/resources/%E2%96%91-crackshotplus-%E2%96%91-1-8-x-1-16-x-%E2%96%91.18085/ Movecraft (free) https://www.spigotmc.org/resources/movecraft.31321/ This plugin allows you to build your own vehicles and pilot them! We have added these four custom vehicles to the server. They require licenses to pilot. Car (Max Size: 500 blocks) Boat (Max Size: 500 blocks) Tank (Max Size: 5,000 blocks) Ship (Max Size: 10,000 blocks) Plane (Max Size: 5,000 blocks) Submarine (Max Size: 5,000 blocks) LaunchTorpedo (Made of Coal blocks, only works underwater!) Movecraft Requirements: 1% of blocks must be Iron Blocks to represent Engines Must have Furnaces with coal in them to represent Fuel Place a sign with the type of vehicle on the top line Use a stick to drive Put a sign that says [helm] on the top line to create a steering wheel to turn.
